About QR Craft

QR Craft is a versatile QR code generator that allows you to create various types of QR codes including contact information, links, emails, phone numbers, SMS, WhatsApp messages, and Wi-Fi credentials. Simply select the type of QR code you need, fill in the details, and craft your custom QR code in seconds! - Ammar :D
